How We Help Holocaust Survivors

The Blue Card provides direct financial assistance, essential services, and life-enhancing programs to Holocaust survivors in need. Our mission is simple: ensure dignity, safety, and quality of life for those who endured the unimaginable.

Emergency Cash Assistance

One-time grants for urgent needs that directly impact a survivor’s safety and living conditions.

  • Prevent eviction and utility shut-offs
  • Emergency relocation and housing repairs
  • Food, clothing, and essential appliances
  • Medical, dental, and burial expenses

Medication Assistance Grant

Financial support to ensure survivors never have to skip lifesaving medications due to cost.

Many survivors live on limited income and are forced to ration prescriptions. This program removes that burden and ensures consistent care.

Learn More →

Dental & Medical Assistance

One-time support to improve physical health and address urgent medical needs.

  • Hearing aids
  • Dental and denture care
  • Medical expenses not covered by insurance
Learn More →

Emergency Response System (TERS)

A 24/7 emergency alert system that allows survivors to remain safely in their homes.

With the press of a button, survivors are instantly connected to care specialists who can dispatch help immediately.

Learn More →

Natural Disaster Relief

Rapid-response aid for survivors affected by hurricanes, fires, and extreme weather events.

Provides food, medical supplies, and emergency support during crises across the country.

Ongoing Monthly Assistance

Recurring financial support to help survivors afford everyday essentials.

  • Food and basic supplies
  • Rent assistance

Fighting Cancer Together Program

Targeted support for survivors battling cancer, who face significantly higher risk rates.

Covers medical co-pays, transportation, and dietary needs during treatment.

Health & Wellbeing Programs

Programs designed to improve emotional, mental, and physical wellbeing.

  • Holiday support and birthday recognition
  • Light therapy for depression (SAD)

  • Summer retreats and nutrition support
