New Editorial Feature

The Longevity Paradox of Holocaust Survivors

Holocaust survivors often live longer than average, yet face higher rates of chronic disease and financial hardship. This episode explains that paradox and shows how The Blue Card provides direct assistance to help cover medical costs, daily needs, and essential support as this population ages.
